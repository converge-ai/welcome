About

It's not enough to simply build and deploy an algorithm. Effective applications require an understanding of how each individual experiences and interacts with a service. We begin every project with this in mind; developing a qualitative understanding of a topic that informs our design process.

Our team has more than 30 years of combined experience in machine learning, marketing, and development.

David

David has been working as a Data Scientist for over 7 years with experience in government, e-commerce, and marketing. While at IBM he developed a fraud detection system for the US government that continues to save over 9 Billion USD annually. Most recently he worked with Rakuten to improve navigation on their web site - implementing a new item ranking and improved discovery. He also holds 3 patents related to natural language processing, and has co-authored a paper on semantic analysis in e-commerce.

Jared

Jared has five years experience in data-driven brand strategy and two years experience as a data scientist in e-commerce. As a brand strategist, he designed and conducted market research - using a combination of ethnography and data analysis of survey, social media, and customer data - that helped clients like Bank of America, TJ Maxx, Dunkin Donuts, and Liberty Mutual Insurance develop new multi-channel advertising campaigns and CSR initiatives that increase brand awareness, recall, and affinity. More recently he has worked at Rakuten to build machine learning solutions at scale for improved personalization, customer segmentation, and marketing automation.

Theang

Theang holds a Ph.D. degree in Applied Mathematics and Physics from Kyoto University (Japan). He has published a dozen of papers in international journals and conference proceedings of Machine Learning, Deep Learning, Data Mining, Evolutionary Computation and Operations Research. He serves as referee and program committee member of several international journals and conferences. Theang combines his research background with industry experiences in telecommunications and e-commerce. Presently, he is lead data scientist for Rakuten Inc. where his work ranges from designing recommender systems, deriving actionable insights out of customer behavior data, to managing company-wide strategic analyses
